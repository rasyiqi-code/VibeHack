The Mission

Advancing the horizon of autonomous security simulation through agentic intelligence.

Why VibeHack?

In the rapidly evolving landscape of cybersecurity, traditional automated scanners often miss the contextual nuances of complex attack surfaces. Human experts can see these nuances but are limited by scale.

VibeHack was born to bridge this gap. We believe that security simulation should be as dynamic and adaptive as the adversaries we defend against.

Stateful Tactical Persistence

Core Philosophy

  • Agentic First: Decisions are driven by goals, not just static scripts.
  • Stateful Heart: Every shell command lives in a persistent environment.
  • Human-in-the-Loop: AI explores, humans authorize. Safety at every step.

Ethical Framework

VibeHack is strictly intended for authorized security auditing. Our mission is to empower red-teams and developers to secure their infrastructure. Any misuse of this tool for unauthorized activities is strictly against the project's governance.
